Low Vision Program

The Need for Low Vision Care

Currently over 50 million people in North America have difficulty seeing even with eyeglasses or contacts according to the most recent CDC (Centers for Disease Control & Prevention) National Health Interview Survey, and that number continues to grow.

As an eyecare professional, you likely see a number of these patients, ones whose vision loss is due to macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy and other eye diseases, and wish you could help them perform everyday tasks and hobbies more easily. Drugs or surgery are available for a number of these eye diseases, but they are often not enough to allow patients to read the newspaper or pill bottles, watch television or enjoy hobbies like solving crossword puzzles.

With Eschenbach’s Low Vision Program, you can help them accomplish these tasks!

The Solution

Our Low Vision Program is a turnkey solution that allows you to easily and efficiently find and offer magnification and vision-enhancing solutions to your visually impaired patients to help them meet their visual goals. With our all-inclusive program, you'll be able to send your patient home the same day with a solution that allows them to more easily perform their daily living tasks like reading, writing and watching television.

Our Low Vision Program features 3 essential components:

1
In-office Training:

A local Eschenbach Territory Manager will come to your practice to conduct extensive training for you and/or your staff on how to quickly and effectively select, demonstrate and dispense the appropriate low vision devices for a patient. They'll also provide practice management tips on how to conduct an exam in a quick and efficient manner.

2
Consultative Support:

You’ll be provided with a variety of tools including patient screening materials, third-party reimbursement guidance, marketing support and other resources to help ensure your low vision care service is successful from the business side.

3
A Diagnostic Dispensing System:

We’ll create a customized assortment of diagnostic devices to meet the specific needs of your patient population—complete with a sleek, professional-grade cabinet that we’ll install for you!
